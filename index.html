About
Graphic design student based in Gent, Belgium.
I have experience in creating digital campaigns, responsive websites, photographs, mobile apps and printed materials.

I'm very passionate about clean and intuitive user interface design, great use of typography, and emerging platforms. Whether it be a website or social campaign, I love being able to build brands and shape how people experience those brands across a variety of media.

I’m always interested in creative collaboration, so feel free to contact me if you’d like to work on a project together.
